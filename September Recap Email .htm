IUSA Event 1 October 4th-9th 

There's no rest for the wicked as the very next weekend UCDSC were on the road again. This time to

Carlingford Lough Yacht Club, who were gracious to host us at their intervarsity team-racing event.

Inter-varsity team-racing is back with a bang for the 2024/5 season. After a long weekend of challenging

wind conditions, lots of rain and tight racing UCD came out on top! Congratulations to the UCD 1s who've

only gone and won the whole event! Can confirm the post event blues took a while to go after this one…

 

 

After all that business we're looking forward to getting back to training this week, as sadly last weekend training had to be

 cancelled due to the high winds :((

 

The sign-up link for this weekend's training will been posted in the groupchat, capacity is limited so those who can't go

this weekend never fear we'll see you next Saturday !
